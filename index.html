HTML LEARNING ROADMAP

This folder groups the main HTML topics into practical lessons so related ideas are learned together instead of being split into too many small folders.

Lesson 1 teaches headings. Lesson 2 teaches paragraphs. Lesson 3 teaches void elements such as <br>, <hr>, <img>, <input>, <meta>, and <link>.

Lesson 4 explains the basic HTML structure and boilerplate. Lesson 5 covers text formatting tags. Lesson 6 combines lists and links because both are common ways to organize and move through content.

Lesson 7 covers images and multimedia. Lesson 8 focuses on tables. Lesson 9 groups forms and inputs because they work together when collecting data from users.

Lesson 10 explains semantic layout tags. Lesson 11 covers the head section and metadata. Lesson 12 combines attributes and entities because both help control how content behaves and displays.

Lesson 13 teaches embedding and graphics such as <iframe>, SVG, and canvas. Lesson 14 introduces interactive elements like <details>, <dialog>, <progress>, and <meter>.

Lesson 15 combines accessibility, SEO, and best practices because these ideas improve HTML quality across every page you build.
