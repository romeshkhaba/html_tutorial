HTML PARAGRAPHS

A paragraph is used to display a block of text that belongs to one main idea. In HTML, paragraphs are created with the <p> tag.

Browsers automatically add space before and after each paragraph, which makes long text easier to read and separates one idea from another.

Paragraph tags are commonly used for explanations, articles, instructions, notes, and any text content that should be read as a complete thought.
